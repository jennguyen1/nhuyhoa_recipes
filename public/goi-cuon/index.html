Goi Cuon (Spring Rolls)

Entree Vietnamese Vegetarianish

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
bun pork belly cucumber
hoisin sauce shrimp lettuce
peanut butter mint
rice paper thai basil
salt


Instructions

instructions
Boil pork belly for a few minutes and then dump liquid
Boil pork belly with salt on medium high heat for ~30 min
Boil shell-on shrimp on medium heat until pink, then remove shell
Slice pork belly and shrimp
Prepare the bun
Prepare the veggies
To make sauce, dilute hoisin sauce & peanut butter with broth until the taste is right
Make rolls
Made with Hugo & Vanilla
