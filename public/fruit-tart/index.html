Fruit Tart

Dessert Vegetarianish

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
butter any
cinnamon
cornstarch
eggs
flour
graham crackers
salt
sugar
vanilla
whole milk


Instructions

instructions
To make crust, mix 1 pack of graham cracker crumbs, 0.25 c sugar, 0.5 tsp cinnamon, and pinch of salt
Slowly stir in 4 tbsp of melted butter
Ensure mixture has consistency of cookie dough (not too dry or wet) by adding more butter or cracker
Press mixture into pan
Freeze for 1 hr or bake at 325F for 7 min, cool and refridgerate before filling
To make creme, mix to a smooth paste 4 tsp sugar and 1 egg with 2 tsp flour and 2 tsp cornstarch
Bring 0.4 c milk to just a boil
Slowly add heated milk to egg mixture, whisking constantly to prevent curdling
Pour mixture into saucepan and cook over medium heat until boiling, whisking constantly
When it boils, whisk until thick
Remove from heat and whisk in 2 tsp of butter and 0.33 tsp vanilla
For a flavored creme, replace vanilla with pureed flavor
Pour into clean bowl, cover with plastic wrap with a few holes poked in
Refridgerate for 2 hours before using
Whisk custard before using to remove any lumps
Assemble with crust, custard, and fruit
