--- title: "Shrimp Broccoli Stir Fry" tags: ['Entree'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
garlic powder shrimp broccoli
oyster sauce carrot
pepper garlic
rice vinegar ginger
salt


Instructions

instructions
Season shrimp with salt, pepper, garlic powder
Steam broccoli carrots in any day for 1 min
Saute garlic and ginger in wok
Stir fry shrimp until pink
Stir in broccoli and carrots
Drizzle in a mixture of 4 oyster : 1 rice vinegar plus water
