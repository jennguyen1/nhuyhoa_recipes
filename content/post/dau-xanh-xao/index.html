--- title: "Dau Xanh Xao (Green Bean Stir Fry)" tags: ['Entree', 'Vietnamese', 'Vegetarianish'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
oyster sauce garlic
pepper green beans
salt hanh kho
mushroom
onion
shallot


Instructions

instructions
Steam green beans for about half the cook time
Saute minced shallots and garlic until slightly golden
Stir in green beans, mushrooms, and sliced onion until soft
Drizzle in diluted oyster sauce (1:1 ratio)
