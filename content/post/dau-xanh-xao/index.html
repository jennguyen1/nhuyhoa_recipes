--- title: "Dau Xanh Xao (Green Bean Stir Fry)" tags: ['Entree', 'Vietnamese'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
oyster sauce garlic
pepper green beans
salt mushroom
shallot


Instructions

instructions
Golden shallots and garlic
Stir in and cook mushrooms and green beans until soft
Drizzle in diluted oyster sauce (1:1 ratio)
