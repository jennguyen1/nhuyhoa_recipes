Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
nuoc mam pha chicken ginger


Instructions

instructions
Boil deboned chicken thighs under a light boil until internal temperature hits 165F
Refrigerate chicken
Eat with nuoc mam cha mixed with ginger
