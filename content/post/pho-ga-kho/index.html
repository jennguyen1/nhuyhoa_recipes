--- title: "Pho Ga Kho (Dry Chicken Pho)" tags: ['Entree', 'Vietnamese'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
chicken boullion chicken thigh bean sprouts
cinnamon stick cilantro
cloves ginger
fried shallot green onion
lime juice onion
mushroom seasoning shallot
pho thai basil
soy sauce
star anise
sugar
turmeric powder


Instructions

instructions
Season chicken thighs with turmeric, salt & pepper
Boil chicken with ginger, onion, lime leaf until cooked
Save chicken broth to use for sauce and to drink as bone broth
Golden shallots
Add couple spoons of chicken broth and add soy sauce, sugar, chicken boullion, mushroom seasonings, and pho spices (cinnamon stick, star anise, cloves), want a strong salty umami flavor
Simmer for a few min
Adjust to taste
Eat with pho, shredded chicken, bean sprouts, thai basil, cilantro, green onion, fried shallot, sauce, and a squeeze of lime juice
