--- title: "Dau Sot Ca Chua (Fried Tofu with Tomato Sauce)" tags: ['Entree', 'Vietnamese', 'Vegetarianish'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
nuoc mam tofu green onions
onion
tomato


Instructions

instructions
Fry tofu
Heat yellow onion and tomatoes in pot on medium heat
When tomatoes have softened, add very small bowl of water
Add fried tofu
Add with nuoc mam and water for flavor
Simmer for 15 minutes
Add green onions in last few minutes
