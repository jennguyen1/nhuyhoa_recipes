--- title: "Mi Vit Quay (Roast Duck Noodle Soup)" tags: ['Entree', 'Vietnamese'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
chicken broth roast duck chives
mi hanh kho
wonton soup base lettuce
mustard greens


Instructions

instructions
Buy a whole roast duck at a Chinese barbecue restaurant
Cut up the roast duck, save the bony parts for the broth and refrigerate the rest
Fill large pot 1/4 full with water, add bony duck parts, and sauce (comes with duck)
When the broth starts to boil, fill 3/4 pot with chicken broth and add wonton soup base
Want the broth to maintain a light boil
Wash and cut veggies
Boil the lettuce and mustard greens
Stir fry washed/dried mi until it is a little crunchy, sprinkle with some soy sauce and chives
Eat with mi, veggies, roast duck, broth, and hanh kho
