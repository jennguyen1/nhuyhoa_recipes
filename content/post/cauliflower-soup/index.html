Instructions

instructions
Plan for 1 large onion per half a head of cauliflower
Caramelize onion & shallots with a sprinkle of salt
Once browned, add minced garlic and ground coriander & cumin
Add cauliflower and saut√© for light color (or use roasted / pan fried cauliflower)
Add chicken broth to just cover, adjust to taste
Simmer until cauliflower is cooked (15-20 min)
Cauliflower total cook time 30 min
Cool then blend with a spoon of yogurt until smooth
Adjust to taste with salt & pepper
Add a squeeze of lime and zest (any citrus zest)
Garnish with chopped herbs (cilantro, dill, mint, chives, etc) and gold raisins
Eat with bread
