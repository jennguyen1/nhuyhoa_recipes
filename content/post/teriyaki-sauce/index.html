--- title: "Teriyaki Sauce" tags: ['Other'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
mirin garlic
sake ginger
soy sauce onion
sugar shallot


Instructions

instructions
Mix teriyaki sauce: ratio 2 soy sauce : 2 sake : 2 mirin : 1 sugar
Bring mixture to a boil and continuously stir on medium low
Simmer for 10-15 min or until sauce is thickened
When small bubbles start to rise/appear when mixing, pour sauce in glass jar, cool, and refridgerate for 2-3 weeks
