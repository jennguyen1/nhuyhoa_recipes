--- title: "Roast Sweet Potato" tags: ['Entree', 'Vegetarianish'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
Kerrygold butter japanese sweet potato
Penzeys Paris Seasoning onion
garlic powder
white miso


Instructions

instructions
Preheat oven to 450F
Poke the japanese sweet potato with a fork
Bake for 30-45 min
Make miso butter by 1 miso : 2 butter (unsalted)
Steam sliced onions in microwave with miso butter for 10 min
Spread miso butter on roast sweet potato with paris seasoning / garlic powder
Eat roast potato with onions
