Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
butter beef ribeye steak garlic
garlic powder shallot
msg
pepper
salt


Instructions

instructions
Rub seasonings on 1.5-2 inch prime steak
Roast steak in oven at 250F until steak reads 105F, approximately 25 min
Before steak comes out of oven, heat cast iron pan with some oil under high heat
Add steak and butter and sear each side for about 1 min to create a crust
Spoon butter onto steak with other aromatics
Use tongs to sear the edges
Serve right away
