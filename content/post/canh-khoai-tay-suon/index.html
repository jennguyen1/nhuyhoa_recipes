--- title: "Canh Khoai Tay Suon (Taro and Pork Rib Soup)" tags: ['Entree', 'Vietnamese', 'Soup', 'Vegetarianish'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
chicken broth pork rib tips japanese sweet potato
macaroni rice paddy herbs
pepper shallot
salt taro
wonton soup base


Instructions

instructions
Marinate the pork rib tips with salt and pepper
Golden minced shallots
Add pork and sear both sides
Add water or chicken broth and adjust to medium high heat
Add sweet potato and boil until cooked
If cooking with taro, boil in a separate pot due to shorter cooking time
Add wonton soup base, adjust to taste
Add rice paddy herbs (ngo om) in the last few minutes
Eat with rice or nui
