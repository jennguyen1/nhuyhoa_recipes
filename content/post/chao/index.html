--- title: "Chao (Porridge)" tags: ['Entree', 'Vietnamese', 'Vegetarianish', 'Soup'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
gio chao quay chicken green onions
jasmine rice shallot
pepper
salt
wonton soup base


Instructions

instructions
Marinate chicken with salt, pepper & shallots
Make rice
Boil chicken until sufficient internal temp
Add rice and boil until it has expanded (~1 hr)
Add wonton soup base
Eat with pepper, green onions, shredded chicken, gio chao quay
