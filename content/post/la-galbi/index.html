--- title: "LA Galbi (Korean Grilled Short Ribs)" tags: ['Entree'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
mirin beef short ribs garlic asian pear
pepper ginger
sesame oil onion
soy sauce
sugar


Instructions

instructions
Marinade: ratio of 1 soy : 0.5 sugar : 0.5 mirin : 1 water, dash of sesame oil, blended korean pear, garlic, onion, ginger, and pinch of pepper
Marinate short ribs (thinly sliced) overnight
Grill or broil 2 min each side + 1 extra min for a total of 5 min
Internal temp of meat should register medium well
