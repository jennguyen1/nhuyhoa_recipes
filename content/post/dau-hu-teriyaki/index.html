--- title: "Dau Hu Teriyaki (Teriyaki Tofu)" tags: ['Entree', 'Vegetarianish'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
teriyaki sauce tofu


Instructions

instructions
Fry tofu
Set tofu aside and allow oil to drain
Simmer teriyaki sauce on medium heat until the sauce becomes thick
Add tofu and coat with sauce
