--- title: "Bo Bia (Spring Rolls with Chinese Sausage)" tags: ['Entree', 'Vietnamese'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
bun eggs cucumber
hoisin sauce lap xuong (chinese pork sausages) lettuce
peanut butter mint
rice paper thai basil


Instructions

instructions
Boil lap xuong for 15 min, move to ice bath when done
Make thin egg omeletes, one egg at a time
Slice sausages and eggs
Prepare the bun
Prepare the veggies
To make sauce, dilute hoisin sauce & peanut butter with broth until the taste is right
Make rolls
