--- title: "Eggs Omelette" tags: ['Entree', 'Vegetarianish'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
mien eggs dried fungus mushrooms
nuoc mam pha ground pork onion
pepper shallot
salt
soy sauce


Instructions

instructions
Soak mien and a few fungus separately in boiling water for a couple minutes, drain, rinse and repeat
Combine eggs and mixed fillings
Stir fry shallots until golden
Add egg mixture
Cook both sides until golden
Serve with nuoc mam pha
