--- title: "Canh Cha Ca (Fish Cake Soup)" tags: ['Entree', 'Vietnamese'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
nuoc mam cha ca (fish paste) dill
wonton soup base green onions
onion
shallot
tomato


Instructions

instructions
Heat yellow onion, shallots, and tomatoes in pot on medium heat
When tomatoes have softened, add 1 bowl of water
Add cha ca
Add wonton soup base and nuoc mam, adjust to taste
Simmer for 15 minutes
Add dill and green onions in the last few minutes
