Instructions

instructions
Dry mix: 1 cup flour, 1.5 tbsp sugar, 1 tsp baking powder, 0.5 tsp baking soda, 0.5 tsp salt
Mix 0.75 c non-dairy milk with 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar and let it sit for 5 min
Wet mix: milk/vinegar mixture, 1 egg, 2 tbsp melted butter, 0.25 tsp vanilla
Combine the wet and dry ingredients, do not stir too much, want a lumpy batter
Cook on a cast iron pan, only flip once
Eat with maple syrup
