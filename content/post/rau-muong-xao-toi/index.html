--- title: "Rau Muong Xao Toi (Stir Fried Water Spinach)" tags: ['Entree', 'Vietnamese', 'Vegetarianish'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
mam tom garlic
shallot
water spinach


Instructions

instructions
Blanche water spinach (rau muong, ong choy)
Golden minced shallots and garlic
Add in water spinach
Stir in diluted mam tom
Stir fry for few minutes
