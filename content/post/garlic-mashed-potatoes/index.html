Instructions

instructions
Garlic Mashed Potatoes
Boil potatoes
Fry garlic until golden
Mash potatoes while stirring in milk, butter, salt, pepper, and garlic
Adjust to taste
Gravy
Heat 3 tbsp butter and shallots until soft
Slowly stir in 2 tbsp of flour
Slowly stir in 6 c chicken broth and 3 tbsp bourbon
Simmer and reduce by half
Add 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar and 3 c milk
Simmer and reduce until thick (add cornstarch if necessary)
Season with salt and pepper
Hot Honey Sauce
Make spice mix ratio of 2 tomato powder : 2 paprika : 1 mustard powder : 1 cumin : 1 sugar : 1 salt
Make sauce with ratio 1 spice mix : 2 apple cider vinegar : 6 honey and adjust to with honey
