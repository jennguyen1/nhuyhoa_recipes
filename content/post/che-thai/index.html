--- title: "Che Thai (Vietnamese Fruit Cocktail)" tags: ['Dessert', 'Vietnamese', 'Vegetarianish'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
amyl acetate flavoring essence jackfruit
half & half longan
nata de coco


Instructions

instructions
Cut the jackfruit into strips
Combine longan, jackfruit, and nata de coco in a big container
Add all of syrup from fruit cans (all of longan and some of jackfruit as needed) and 2 cups of half & half
Add 1 drop of amyl acetate flavoring essence
Mix well and serve with crushed ice
