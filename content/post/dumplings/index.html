--- title: "Dumplings" tags: ['Entree'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
pepper ground pork green onions
salt shrimp shallot
soy sauce water chestnuts
sugar
vinegar
wonton wrappers


Instructions

instructions
Mix ground pork (66% lean) and loosely ground shrimp at a 1:2 ratio
Mix in minched shallots, water chestnuts, salt, and pepper
Wrap small spoonful of mix into circular wrappers (Twin Marquis brand), dumpling or siu mai style
Fry: fry both sides of potstickers, add a small bowl of water and cover to steam
Boil: add to a pot of boiling water, once it has floated to the surface, cook for 2-3 more min
Steam: steam for 10 min
Sauce: stir fry shallots, lower from heat and add soy sauce, water, vinegar, sugar, and adjust to taste
