--- title: "Banh Day (Vietnamese Sticky Rice Cake)" tags: ['Dessert', 'Vietnamese'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
bot nep cha
oil


Instructions

instructions
Combine bot nep (glutinous rice flour) slowly with about 400 ml of water and about 1/3 cup of oil
Mix dough; should be hard, not too soft
Knead with hands
Form little balls, a bit smaller than a stress ball
Place on aluminum foil squares
Steam for 20 min
Sandwich cha between two rice cakes
