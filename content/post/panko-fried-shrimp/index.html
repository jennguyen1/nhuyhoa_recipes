--- title: "Panko Fried Shrimp" tags: ['Entree'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
flour eggs
panko bread crumbs shrimp
pepper
salt


Instructions

instructions
Remove shell except tail from shrimp
Season shrimp with salt & pepper
Batter mixes: (1) whipped egg with water, (2) flour, (3) panko bread crumbs
Dip shrimp in egg mixture, flour, egg mixture, panko mix
Pan fry with moderate layer of oil until golden on both sides
