--- title: "Mango Sticky Rice" tags: ['Dessert', 'Vegetarianish'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
coconut milk mango
salt
sugar
sweet rice


Instructions

instructions
Rinse and soak sweet rice in cold water overnight
Cook sweet rice in rice cooker
Make coconut sauce by heating half can of coconut milk with 3 sugar : 1 salt mixture and a starchy slurry, adjust to taste
Soak xoi with coconut sauce and let it sit for 30 min before serving
Serve with cold mangoes
