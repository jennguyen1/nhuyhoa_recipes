--- title: "Banh Flan Ca Phe" tags: ['Dessert', 'Vietnamese'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
Cafe Du Monde coffee eggs
Longevity sweetened condensed milk
sugar
whole milk


Instructions

instructions
Put 4 spoons of coffee into a coffee press
Pour boiling water into pressor and refill for a total of 3 times
Let coffee cool for several hours
Stir in milk, want a ratio 2 coffee : 1 milk
Caramelize sugar with a little water until golden and coat over cake pan/walls
Crack and beat 8 eggs at room temperature
Stir in 2-3 spoons of sugar
Stir in 1 can of longevity sweetened condensed milk
Stir in coffee
Pour into caramelized coated cake pan
Steam for 20-25 min, want a light boil
Turn off and let cake sit in steamer for 5 min
Set out to cool and then refrigerate
If more coffee desired, pour it onto cake after it has cooled
