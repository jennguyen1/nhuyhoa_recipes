--- title: "Ca Chien (Fried Fish)" tags: ['Entree', 'Vietnamese'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
nuoc mam pha catfish garlic
pepper shallot
salt


Instructions

instructions
Cut catfish approximately 3 in long
Marinate fish in salt, pepper, minced shallots and garlic
Place in fridge for a half hour
Fry both sides of fish on medium heat
Eat with nuoc mam pha
