--- title: "Com Chien (Fried Rice)" tags: ['Entree', 'Vietnamese', 'Vegetarianish'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
chicken powder eggs garlic any
pepper any onion
rice shallot
salt any
soy sauce


Instructions

instructions
Make rice with less rice or use day-old rice that is dry
Prepare and cook the meat and veggies, drying thoroughly
Golden shallots, garlic, onions
Add eggs on low power
Add salt, pepper, soy sauce
Scramble eggs and add moderate amount of rice
Stir fry rice and add seasonings (salt, pepper, soy sauce, chicken powder) to liking
Once rice is done (fried and toasty), stir in meat and veggies
