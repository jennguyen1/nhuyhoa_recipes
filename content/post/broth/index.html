Instructions

instructions
Blanche meat with salt water and vinegar for 10-15 min, then rinse thoroughly to remove impurities
Sear aromatics without oil and transfer to pot with heated shallot/garlic oil
Add meat on top of aromatics and fill water to slightly cover meat
Bring to a boil and lower to soft simmer, periodically skimming foam
Add toasted spices and small amount of salty seasonings
Simmer to 1-4 hours, removing meat at appropriate times to prevent overcooking
Adjust to taste (2 salty : 1 sweet ratio, msg seasonings)
Strain broth and set at room temp to cool
