Instructions

instructions
Blanche meat with salt water and vinegar for 10-15 min, then rinse thoroughly to remove impurities
Fill pot with water to just cover meat ~1 in
Bring to a boil and then lower to a soft simmer
Periodically skim the foam
Add aromatics
Simmer for 2-4 hours
Adjust to taste
Strain broth and set at room temp to cool
