Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
banh canh chicken bones bean sprouts
fish sauce chicken feet chives
msg chicken wings cilantro
pepper dried shrimp daikon
salt dried squid garlic
sugar pork belly ginger
wonton soup base pork bones green onions
pork feet hanh kho
pork hock onion
shrimp shallot
vietnamese mint


Instructions

instructions
Make broth with pork bones and feet/hock, chicken, shrimp heads/shell, dried shrimp, dried squid, and aromatics (daikon, onion, shallot, garlic, ginger)
Add fish sauce/salt and sugar at a 2 salty : 1 sweet ratio, adjust to taste with wonton soup base, msg , and pepper
Boil shrimp and pork belly, save pork feet from broth
Mince and mix green onions, cilantro and Vietnamese mint/coriander (rau ram)
Prep and cut chives & bean sprouts
Boil banh canh noodles
Eat with banh canh, meat & veggies, and hanh kho
