--- title: "Sweet Chili Sauce" tags: ['Other'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
potato starch chili pepper
salt garlic
sugar
vinegar


Instructions

instructions
Blend 1 medium chili pepper, 8 cloves garlic, and 0.33c water until there are no big chunks
Transfer mixture to pot
Add 1c sugar, 0.75c vinegar, and pinch of salt
Simmer for 5 min
Add 1 tbsp of potato starch (make a slurry) and stir until it boils
Cool, transfer to jar, and store in fridge
