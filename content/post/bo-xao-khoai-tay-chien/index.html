--- title: "Bo Xao Khoai Tay Chien (Beef and French Fries Stir Fry)" tags: ['Entree', 'Vietnamese'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
oyster sauce beef garlic
pepper onion
salt shallot


Instructions

instructions
Marinate sliced beef with salt, pepper, minced shallots, garlic, and a pinch of oil
Fry potatoes
Stir fry beef until cooked but moist (remove excess liquid)
Add potatoes and sliced onions
Drizzle in diluted oyster sauce (1:1 ratio)
