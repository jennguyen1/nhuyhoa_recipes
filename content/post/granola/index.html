Instructions

instructions
Preheat to 325F
Want to maintain between 6:1 to 5:1 dry to wet ingredients
Mix 2 c oats, 1 c rice crispies, 0.5 c almonds, 1 c crushed pita chips, 3 tbsp flax seeds, and 0.5 tsp salt
Mix in 0.16 c honey, 0.16 c brown sugar, 0.33 c oil, 1 tbsp vanilla
Transfer mixture to parchment paper lined baking sheet and spread into an even, compressed layer
Bake until lightly brown 20-25 min, rotating half way
Cool at room temperature for 1 hour then break until desired size
Store at room temperature for 2 weeks
