--- title: "Nuoc Mam Pha" tags: ['Other', 'Vietnamese'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
nuoc mam garlic
sugar
vinegar


Instructions

instructions
Add sugar to a large bowl and level out
Pour vinegar into the bowl, slightly over the surface of the sugar
Add nuoc mam, about 1/3 of current level
Mix in water to dilute
Adjust to taste, should be sweet and sour, not salty
Mix in minced garlic
