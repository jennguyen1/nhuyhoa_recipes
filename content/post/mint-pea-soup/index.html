--- title: "Mint Pea Soup" tags: ['Entree', 'Soup', 'Vegetarianish'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
chicken broth garlic
ground coriander mint
ground cumin onion
pepper pea
salt shallot
yogurt


Instructions

instructions
Caramelize onion and shallots with a sprinkle of salt
Once browned, add minced garlic and ground coriander & cumin
Add peas and chicken broth to just cover, adjust to taste
Simmer until peas are cooked (2-4 min)
Cool then blend with mints and a spoon of yogurt until smooth
Adjust to taste with salt & pepper
Garnish with sliced mint
