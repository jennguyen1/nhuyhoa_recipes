--- title: "Ga Ca Ri (Vietnamese Chicken Curry)" tags: ['Entree', 'Vietnamese'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
baguette chicken carrot
chicken broth potato
chicken powder shallot
curry powder sweet potato
pepper yam
salt
wonton soup base


Instructions

instructions
Cut chicken legs/thighs into pieces
Marinate chicken with salt, pepper, shallots, 1 spoon of curry powder, wonton soup base or chicken powder, and oil
Refrigerate for few hours or overnight
Golden shallots
Stir fry chicken
Stir in carrots, potatoes, yam, sweet potato
Add a ratio of 1 chicken broth : 1 water to just cover chicken
Simmer on med low for at least 30 min, adjust to taste
Eat with baguette
