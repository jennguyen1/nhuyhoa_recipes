--- title: "Dua Chua (Pickled Mustard Greens)" tags: ['Entree', 'Vietnamese', 'Vegetarianish'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
brown sugar green onions
salt mustard greens


Instructions

instructions
Cut mustard greens
Using the white parts of the green onions, cut into quarter circles
Mix 1.75 tbsp brown sugar, 1 tbsp salt, and 1L of warm water
Before adding the salt and water, squeeze the brown sugar and green onions
Taste mixture, should be sweet and sour
Add mustard green leaves
Squeeze the mixture
Pour mixture into a container and tightly seal
Store in a warm place for a few days
Try a piece, want it to be sweet and sour
Place in fridge when it has the desired taste
