--- title: "Arroz Con Pollo" tags: ['Entree'] ---

Ingredients

Other Meat Veggie Fruit
beer chicken carrot
pepper cilantro
rice garlic
salt onion
peas


Instructions

instructions
Blend cilantro, onion, garlic, oil and beer to make marinade
Marinate chicken with salt, pepper, and marinade
Clean rice and mix in some salt, marinade, and beer
Cook rice, stir it when done to distribute the greens
Fry the chicken
Eat rice with chicken and steamed peas and carrots
