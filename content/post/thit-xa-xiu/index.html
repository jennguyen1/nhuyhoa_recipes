Instructions

instructions
Marinade: 0.5 tsp pepper, 2 tsp five spice, 2 tbsp sugar, 2 tbsp honey, 1 tsp sesame oil, 1 tbsp oyster sauce, 2 tbsp hoisin sauce, 1 tbsp shaoxing wine, 1.5 tbsp soy sauce, 2 tbsp fermented bean curd, 3 garlic cloves or garlic powder, pinch of msg
Cut pork shoulder couple inches wide and 1 inch thick, pork with fork
Set a couple spoons of marinade aside and place in fridge
Marinate pork for 1-2 days
Basting liquid: honey + water
Roast at 400F until internal temperature of 150F (~45 min) basting midway through
Do a final baste with the sauce that was set aside
Eat with rice and heated/thickened sauce or in other dishes
